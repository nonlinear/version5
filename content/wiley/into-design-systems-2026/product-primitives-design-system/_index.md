# Product Primitives: The New Material of Design System

---

> 🤖 **Expressive Design Systems** (Yesenia Perez-Cruz's book) - Focuses on creating design systems that enable expression and personality while maintaining consistency. Product primitives are foundational design elements (tokens, patterns, components) that serve as the "raw material" for building expressive interfaces. Unlike rigid component libraries, primitives allow designers to compose unique experiences while staying within system constraints. Key concept: balance between systematic consistency and creative expression. Book advocates for flexible, composable design systems over prescriptive component catalogs.

> 🤖 **Grammar vs Vocabulary in Design Systems:**

---

>

---

> **Grammar** = the rules, patterns, and structure (how things work together). Design system team defines: spacing scale, color token architecture, component composition patterns, layout grids, interaction patterns. These are the foundational rules everyone follows.

---

>

---

> **Vocabulary** = the specific content, domain primitives, and business objects (what things are called, what they represent). Domain/product teams supply: entity names (Customer, Order, Invoice), domain-specific components (InvoiceLineItem, ShipmentTracker), brand-specific tokens (PrimaryActionBlue, WarningAmber).

---

>

---

> Analogy: Grammar = "how to build a sentence." Vocabulary = "the words you use." System provides structure; teams provide meaning.
